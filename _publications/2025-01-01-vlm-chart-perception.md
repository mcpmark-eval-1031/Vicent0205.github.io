Vision-language models (VLMs) often struggle with chart understanding tasks. This paper systematically investigates the bottleneck and finds that the key limiting factor is **chart perception** — the ability to accurately extract visual information from charts — rather than subsequent reasoning. Based on this finding, we propose targeted interventions to address the perception bottleneck and significantly improve VLM performance on chart understanding benchmarks.
