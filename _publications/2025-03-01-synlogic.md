SynLogic is a scalable data synthesis framework for logical reasoning training of large language models. It automatically generates **diverse, rule-based reasoning tasks** — including deductive, inductive, and abductive reasoning — with ground-truth verifiable answers suitable for reinforcement learning (e.g., GRPO/PPO). Experiments demonstrate strong improvements in logical reasoning capabilities and positive transfer to mathematical and general reasoning benchmarks.
