C-Eval is a comprehensive Chinese evaluation suite for foundation models covering **52 diverse disciplines** across four difficulty levels: middle school, high school, college, and professional. It provides a rigorous benchmark for assessing the Chinese language understanding and reasoning capabilities of large language models.

**Authors:** Yuzhen Huang, Yuzhuo Bai, Zhihao Zhu, Junlei Zhang, Jinghan Zhang, Tangjun Su, **Junteng Liu**, Chuancheng Lv, Yikai Zhang, Jiayi Lei, Yao Fu, Maosong Sun, Junxian He
