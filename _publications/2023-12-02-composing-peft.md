This paper proposes composing parameter-efficient modules (e.g., LoRA adapters) via arithmetic operations such as **addition, negation, and interpolation**. The approach enables flexible model editing, forgetting, generalization, and multi-task learning by treating task-specific modules as vectors in weight space — all without any additional training.

**Authors:** Jinghan Zhang, Shiqi Chen, **Junteng Liu**, Junxian He
